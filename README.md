# Takshashila HR Portfolio

**Takshashila, an ancient centre of learning** — reimagined as a modern HR knowledge base.

This repository is a curated collection of HR frameworks, templates, playbooks, and practical resources across the employee lifecycle. It is designed to showcase structured HR thinking and provide useful tools across recruitment, onboarding, performance management, policies, employee engagement, learning, and people operations.

## What people will find here

- Recruitment frameworks and interview tools
- Onboarding checklists and process documents
- Performance management templates
- Policy and compliance samples
- Learning and development resources
- Employee engagement ideas
- People operations systems
- HR reports, trackers, and practical templates

## Purpose

This repository serves two purposes:
1. To showcase my work and thinking as an HR professional.
2. To build a practical HR knowledge base that organisations and professionals can learn from and use.
